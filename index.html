We deliver landmark projects

We introduce ourselves a company in the field of raw water treatment systems and waste water pollution abetment. Incorporated as a Limited Liability Partnership company in August 2018, we “M M Paryavaran Engineers LLP” have created a niche in the market and are emerging as a leading company in providing consultancy services, design, construction, manufacture, installation and commissioning of a wide range of Sewage Treatment Plants, Effluent Treatment Plants, etc. Having our base at Mohali (Chandigarh) we are providing our services across India. The Company’s promoter Love Kumar is a veteran in the field and widely experienced. An engineer by profession, he started his career way back in the year 1988. Before launching his own company he worked for various big water treatment companies of India.
